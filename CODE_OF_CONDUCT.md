# 🤝 Governance & Institutional Core Standards

The SipHeron ecosystem fundamentally values rigorous open-source architectural implementation, formal verifiable evidence, and respectful interdisciplinary engineering collaboration. This ethical framework governs the operational standards of our interactions.

## Our Axioms

- **Acknowledge Rigorous Construction:** Be relentlessly constructive in all structural evaluations and PR assessments. Review logic, state matrices, and execution variables, separating the codebase from its author.
- **Ecological Diversity:** Welcome contributors of all technical proficiencies, engineering backgrounds, and institutional origins. The best cryptographic concepts are inherently decentralized.
- **Graciously Parse Operational Faults:** Accept functional feedback elegantly. Identify anomalies and deprecate inefficiencies without personal defense architectures.
- **Default Determinism:** Disagree systematically referencing technical proofs. Formulate algorithmic models predicting performance instead of rhetorical defense bounds. 

## Unacceptable Operational Vectors

- Personal degradation, structural insults, or systemic harassment behaviors targeting external actors.
- Discarding Pull Requests, algorithmic modifications, or execution changes without mathematically or functionally explicit evaluations.
- Leaking topological vectors, institutional keys, or private payload definitions spanning community interactions.
